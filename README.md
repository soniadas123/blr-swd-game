# Drain Patrol · Bengaluru 2026

A retro, pixel-art visualization of time-stamped GPS field data from citizen drain audits in Bengaluru.

**[Play it live](https://soniadas123.github.io/blr-swd-game/)**

## What it is

Each dot on the map is a real-world observation collected in the field using **ODK (Open Data Kit)**, an open-source mobile app for recording geo-referenced survey data. Pressing play replays the audits day by day, showing how citizen teams progressively covered three of Bengaluru's natural drainage valleys:

- Vrishabhavathi Valley
- Koramangala–Challaghatta Valley
- Hebbal Valley

## Data source

Citizen audit data from the **Building a Resilient Bengaluru** website (accessed on 23rd May 2026). Basemap tiles courtesy of OpenStreetMap contributors.
